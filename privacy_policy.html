Effective Date: May 15, 2026

Ossdn Apps ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in the Ossdn Smart Calculator mobile application.

Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or share any personal information about you. We do not require you to create an account or provide any contact details to use the application.

Your app preferences (such as Dark/Light mode and Haptic Feedback settings) are stored locally on your device using industry-standard storage mechanisms. You can clear this data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing the app cache.

Our app does not collect any personal information from children. It is a utility tool designed for general audiences.
